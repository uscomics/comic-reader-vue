Origin

Scott White is an American teenager who, when he sleeps, enters a dream world known as Wild World. In Wild World dinosaurs, ape-men, and humans all co-exist side by side. Wild Boy is a Dream Warrior.

The natives of Wild World refer to Scott as Wild Boy. As Wild Boy, Scott is friends with many of the natives, dinosaurs and ape-men. He is also skilled at unarmed combat, combat with with primitive weapons such as knives and spears, hunting, tracking, and in herbal medicines. Wild Boy has a pet Quentzalcoatlus named Keeto. Keeto is large enough to carry Wild Boy on his back when he flys.
