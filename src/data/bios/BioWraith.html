Origin

Police Officers Gary Kennedy and his brother were killed by criminals. Gary vowed to return from the dead to avenge his brother's death. Weeks later, at the witching hour, he rose from his grave as a ghost with super strength when solid, the ability to fly, possess people as a ghost, or become invisible. He also possesses the ability to drain or reinforce the life force of others. The Wraith is a member of the Legends Europe.
