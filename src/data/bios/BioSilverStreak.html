Origin

Nate "Silver" Johnson was chosen as a volunteer in the U.S. Government's secret Æther experiments. By taping into the Æther, they believed a man would be able to move at the speed of light. The experiment was a success. Nate's body was merged with the foam of the Æther. Nate dubbed himself the Silver Streak and, after recieving Camp X training, joined the European branch of the Legends to fight Hitler.
