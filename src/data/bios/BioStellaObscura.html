Origin

Stella Obscura is an alternate universe's version of Luana Jones, the girlfriend of Captain Æther. In her universe Captain Æther was killed and she took a job in a saloon and called herself Stella Obscura.

Superbrain kidnapped and tortured Stella. Using his mental powers, he brought her under his control. His scientists then gave her the ability to generate and control Dark Bio-Mater, a substance that entangles its victims and drains their life force in a matter of minutes. Stella later broke free of Superbrain's mind control.
